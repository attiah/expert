E-HEALTH APPLICATION

The system to be developed will be a mobile-based expert system for a disease diagnosis. The disease symptoms have to be in a specific specialism such as stomach, kidney, and bones Aches, heart attacks, cancers, general infections, joints infections, etc. Diagnostic rules will be taken from the doctors whose specialized in these kind of Diseases. On the other hand, we let the doctors feed our system with data such as: diseases symptoms and treatments and our job to process these data and ask the patient to answer sequential questions we ask him, based on the answers we will diagnose the patient and provide the proper treatments for him. However, the expert system cannot take the doctor position. It just helps to provide free medical diagnose.
